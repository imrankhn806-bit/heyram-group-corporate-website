# Heyram Group Corporate Website – Case Study

## Overview

Heyram Group is a multi-service organization providing solutions across various industries including staffing, immigration consulting, construction, accounting, cleaning services, business support, and technology services.

The objective of this project was to create a professional, responsive, and scalable corporate website that effectively represents the company's diverse services while providing an intuitive user experience for potential clients.

**Live Website:** https://heyram.ca/

---

## Business Challenge

The organization required a centralized digital platform capable of:

- Presenting multiple business services under one brand.
- Providing clear navigation across service categories.
- Improving online visibility and credibility.
- Generating customer inquiries through contact forms.
- Delivering a seamless experience across desktop and mobile devices.

---

## Solution

A responsive corporate website was developed using modern web technologies and CMS capabilities.

Key implementation areas included:

- Responsive website design
- Service-focused landing pages
- WordPress content management integration
- User-friendly navigation structure
- SEO optimization
- Contact and inquiry management
- Cross-browser compatibility
- Performance optimization
